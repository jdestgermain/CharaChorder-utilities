CharaChorder Utilities - Home

  • Calculate Bigram Frequency
    • This tool helps you find two letter chords that are infrequently occuring in words so that you don't accidentally trigger chords.
  • Chord Statistics
    • This tool looks at your uploaded chord map and provides some statistics (number of chords, breakdown by number of letters 2 letter/3 letter ... etc.)
  • Chord Finder
    • This tool looks at your uploaded chord map and a text and finds common words and phrases in the text that you don't have chords for.
  • Partial Anagrams
    • This tool looks at a text and identifies all of the partial anagrams. Partial anagrams (like tough and thought) are usually words that you need special attention to for choosing chord maps since they share the same overall letters.
  • CharaChorder Practice
    • This tool will take your uploaded CSV chord file and either copy a string into your clipboard for practicing in Monkeytype or CharaTyping or let you practice right on the page with chord hints.
  • CharaChorder One Chord Generator
    • This tool will take an uploaded text file of words or list of words typed in and attempt to generate a chord library file for them using a variety of rules. Originally from https://github.com/bittujoju/charachorder
  • Chord Timing Diagnostic Tool
    • Sometimes it can be helpful to try to look closer at the timing that you are pressing and releasing keys to diganose what you might be doing wrong in a chord if it isn't triggering. This tool, developed by Tangent Chang (andy23512) on the CharaChorder Discord, can help with that.
